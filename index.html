Project Summary

The Illawarra TAFE Student Association (ITSA) wanted to have a way for students to upload their documents, have them costed and get it all printed without coming to campus if they didn't have to.

This project started by adding a printing service to the ITSA's existing website. After some discussion with ITSA, it was decided to expand the project's scope to include remaking the ITSA website and helping move hosting to AWS as the printing service needed to use a different hosting site and would end up saving costs for ITSA.
